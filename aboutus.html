NAVIGATE THROUGH THE LAND OF GIANTS WITH LIFE-SIZE DINOSAURS AND ERUPTING VOLCANO

Dino Park Phuket Mini-golf & Restaurant was established in 1997, inspired by the iconic Jurassic Park movies, we collaborated with the creators of the dinosaur scenes from Jurassic Park 1 and 2 to establish the first dinosaur-themed attraction in Thailand and even in Asia. Dino Park Phuket is the most prominent dinosaur-themed attraction in Thailand, highly popular in Phuket, Thailand.

The park, covering over 4 Rai, is situated in the heart of Kata-Karon area in Phuket, Thailand. The park is divided into two zones. The first zone features an 18-hole mini-golf course with a Jurassic theme, surrounded by various species of life-size dinosaurs throughout the 18 holes. Currently, the park has added high standard animatronic dinosaurs that move realistically, bringing an immersive experience to the visitors, as well as a simulated volcanic eruption with state-of-the-art light and sound effects, providing visitors with an immersive and enjoyable atmosphere. Other part is the restaurant area is designed with the concept of Stone Age, you can think about Flintstones cartoon concepts, adding to the unique and enjoyable experience.
